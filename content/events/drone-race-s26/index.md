**Saturday, May 31 · 11 AM – 4 PM · Peavy Field**

The DAM Robotics Club is hosting a drone race on May 31st at Peavy Field from 11am–4pm. Drone racing is a sport where we fly agile quad-rotor drones around an obstacle course of gates and flags. After taking 2nd place at nationals, our team is excited to demonstrate our flying skills on-campus.

## Spectators

DAM Drone Race is open to all spectators. Come watch some fast racing action and learn what's possible with drones!

## Pilots

We also invite FPV drone pilots to race with us.

- **DAM Robotics members** — participate for free.
- **OSU students** — [join DAM Robotics for free](https://discord.com/invite/N9UP5d4Usa) to race at no cost.
- **Non-members** — $10 entry fee, paid the day of the race.

Please register in advance at [MultiGP](https://www.multigp.com/races/view/?race=32043).
